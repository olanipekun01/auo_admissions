Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Where is Achievers University Owo located?

Achievers University Owo is located at KM 1, Idasen- Ute Road, Owo, Ondo State, Nigeria. Owo is about 50km from Akure, the Ondo State Capital, about an hour drive from Benin City and also has direct transport links with Abuja, Lagos, Ibadan and Kogi State
